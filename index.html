About Us

As Southwest Florida's premier collision center, Anthony's Touch of Class takes pride in restoring your vehicle to its original beauty. Located in Port Charlotte, Florida, our experienced team has been delivering quality collision repair and auto body work that drivers across the region trust.

Since opening our doors, Anthony's Touch of Class Collision Center has been the trusted name for premium auto body repair and restoration. Located in the heart of Port Charlotte, we take pride in serving the entire Gulf Coast community, from Sarasota and Bradenton down through North Port and Punta Gorda.

We combine old-school craftsmanship with modern technology to ensure every vehicle that leaves our shop returns to its pre-accident condition. Whether it's a minor dent or major structural repair, our team is dedicated to providing the "Touch of Class" your vehicle deserves.
