About Us

As Southwest Florida's premier collision center, Anthony's Touch of Class takes pride in restoring your vehicle to its original beauty. Located in Port Charlotte, Florida, our experienced team has been delivering quality collision repair and auto body work that drivers across the region trust.

From minor dents and scratches to major collision damage, we handle every job with the same attention to detail and commitment to excellence. We work with all insurance companies to make your repair experience as smooth as possible.

Our state-of-the-art facility and skilled technicians ensure that every vehicle leaving our shop meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
